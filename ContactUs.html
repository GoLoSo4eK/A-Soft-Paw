Frequently Asked Questions

We offer a comprehensive range of services for both cats and dogs, including routine check-ups, vaccinations, dental care, surgery, grooming, behavioral consultations, nutrition counseling, and emergency care. Our team is trained to handle the specific needs of different breeds and ages.

Yes, we offer professional grooming services for all breeds of dogs and cats. Our services include bathing, brushing, coat trimming, nail clipping, ear cleaning, and more. Our groomers are experienced in handling pets with different temperaments and special needs.

We provide 24/7 emergency care for critical situations. Our emergency services include trauma care, poisoning treatment, severe illness management, emergency surgery, and critical care monitoring. During off-hours, we have an on-call veterinarian available through our emergency line at (555) 911-PETS.

We accept various payment methods including cash, credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), pet insurance, CareCredit, and mobile payment options like Apple Pay and Google Pay. We also offer payment plans for certain treatments and services.

Yes, we accept most major pet insurance plans. We can help you file claims and provide all necessary documentation. However, payment is typically required at the time of service, and the insurance company will reimburse you directly. We recommend confirming coverage with your insurance provider before your visit.

We request at least 24 hours' notice for appointment cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee of $25. We understand that emergencies happen, so please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule. Repeated no-shows may affect future appointment scheduling.
